# SBS Community Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, professional and welcoming community for people of every background, identity, role and level of experience.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate with patience, clarity and respect.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Protect confidential production information and personal data.
- Use synthetic examples in public discussions.
- Accept maintainers' decisions while remaining free to disagree constructively.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, publication of private information, deliberate disruption and sustained personal attacks are not accepted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-advisory channel when confidentiality is required. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, lock discussions, reject changes or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and impartially as possible, with information shared only as needed for investigation and resolution.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests and other spaces where a person represents the SBS project.
